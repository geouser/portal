2017 — 2018 Research Stage

The period of 2017-2018 is considered by the Foundation as a research period, during which through grants and it is own artistic programs it will explore Armenian contemporary art scene.

Grant program: Project Grants for Artists
Project Grants for Artists are designed for visual artists or collectives, working in different media, incl. performance art, photography, audio-visual, digital and media art, and art in public spaces.

Artistic program
A year-round artistic program developed by the Curator of the Foundation will include exhibitions, artist meetings, discussions, lectures, workshops, and other public activities.

Besides that the Foundation is planning to create a space as a resource center and dialogue platform.

An open welcoming space that would foster interaction between the art community and general public, become a place for self-education, and stimulate new attitudes towards today’s world. It will provide resources for creative work and research (library on contemporary art, digital resources), and will become a place where different views and perspectives will be presented.

2019 — 2020 Implementation of the Large Scale Projects

The Large Scale Projects based on the results of the Research Period will be developed by the Foundation. There are a projects Foundations is aiming to develop, but we want them to be formed from the “inside” during the period of active research.
