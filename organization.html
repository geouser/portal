Armenia Art Foundation (AA Foundation) is an independent non-profit organization aiming to support the development of contemporary art in Armenia. Based in Yerevan, it was established in 2016 by David Nazaryan and Rafael Nazaryan, and a group of people passionate about Armenian culture.

The mission of the Foundation is to support professionals working in Armenia in the field of contemporary art in Armenia, and to unlock their creative potential both in the country and abroad.

The Armenia Art Foundation supports artists by awarding grants and initiating new art and educational projects in Armenia involving local and international artists, curators, and theorists.

The Armenia Art Foundation seeks to promote contemporary art within Armenia and create a sustainable interest towards it, as well as participate in international cultural exchange initiatives and promote contemporary art from Armenia internationally

Goals of the Armenia Art Foundation Create sustainable conditions for art production and presentation Research and represent the history and current state of contemporary art in Armenia Strongly engage Armenia in an intellectual dialogue with its neighbours and the world as whole Inspire an interest in contemporary art and improve the public’s understanding of it within Armenia
